Creating beauty one nail at a time My Journey

Welcome to my nail journey! My name is Diana and I created this page to share my experience as a beginner nail tech. Even though I am a full-time college student with a busy schedule, I wanted a place where I could showcase my progress and passion for acrylic nails. I may not have had as much time to practice as I would like, but every set represents the growth I've made. Thank you for following along as I continue to learn, create, and improve!

Growing up, I loved getting my nails done, but it was often too expensive to do regularly. When I came to college, I bought my first gel kit and started doing my own nails. However, I really wanted to learn acrylic nails. On September 30, 2023, I did my very first acrylic set. From there, I began practicing on other college girls. Balancing school and practice wasn’t easy, and eventually, I had to pause my regular practice to focus on classes. My dream is to keep growing my skills and eventually become a licensed nail tech!
